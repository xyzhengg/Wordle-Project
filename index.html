WORDLE is a 5 letter word guessing game.

You have 6 tries to guess the word.

Each guess must be a valid 5 letter word.

Press the "Submit" button to submit each guess.

After each guess, the colour of the tiles will change. For example, if the word to guess is "XINYU":

  • If a letter in your guess is in the word but NOT in the right position, it will turn orange
  • If a letter in your guess is in the RIGHT position, the tile will turn green
  • If a letter in your guess is WRONG and not in the word at all, it will turn grey
  • When you've guessed the word, the tiles will all turn green

In this version of WORDLE, you score points based on the number of attempts it takes to guess the word.

Want to make it more challenging? Go to the settings button to make some changes!
