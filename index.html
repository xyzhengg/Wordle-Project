Let's play Wordle!

Instructions

Wordle is a 5 letter word guessing game.

You have 5 tries to guess the word.

Each guess must be a valid 5 letter word.

Press the "Submit" button to submit each guess.

After each guess, the colour of the tiles will change:

  • If a letter in your guess is in the RIGHT position, the tile will turn green
  • If a letter in your guess is in the word but NOT in the right position, it will turn orange
  • If a letter in your guess is WRONG and in the word at all, it will turn grey
